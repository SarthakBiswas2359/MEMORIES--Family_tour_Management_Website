Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, often referred to as the "Paradise on Earth," is a breathtakingly beautiful state in northern India that boasts of stunning landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. The state is famous for its snow-capped mountains, picturesque valleys, serene lakes, and lush greenery. The world-famous Dal Lake in Srinagar is a major tourist attraction, where visitors can enjoy a shikara ride or stay in houseboats. Jammu and Kashmir are also a hub of adventure sports, with activities like skiing, trekking, and white-water rafting being popular. The state is also known for its exquisite handicrafts, mouth-watering cuisine, and ancient temples and shrines. With its mesmerizing beauty, unique cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, Jammu and Kashmir is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for an unforgettable holiday experience.
